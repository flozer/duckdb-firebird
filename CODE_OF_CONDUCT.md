# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project space for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience level, or point of view.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections gracefully.
- Keep technical discussions focused on evidence, reproducible behavior, and
  the project goals.
- Give clear feedback without personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, customer data, or private database content.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions after being asked to stop.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests,
or other contributions that violate this code of conduct. Maintainers may also
temporarily or permanently restrict participation.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the project maintainer. For security
issues, follow `SECURITY.md` instead of opening a public issue.
